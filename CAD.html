Bladeless Fan

My design strategy begins with concept generation and function analysis.
For the bladeless fan, the basic functions were identified as:

  • Multiplying the input air flow rate
  • Producing a directional output flow

The parametric model of the final design was produced in SolidWorks.

A prototype was manufactured to demonstrate functionality.
